Sustainable is Attainable

We believe in minimal intervention, and maximum creativity.

Our Goals

Sustainable Growth

Non-loss, non-dividend enterprises commit to reinvesting all proceeds in their solving their social cause.

The business goal is to create impact, not extract profit.

Minimal Intervention

We believe in providing entrepreneurs with the autonomy to execute their ideas.
No one knows all the answers, but we're here to help find sustainable solutions.

Maximum Impact

While social businesses need to be sustainable, we measure their impact based on their effect on society, not on the profits they create.

Fair Wages

Boots on the ground is where impact happens. In order to attract great talent, social enterprises need the funding to properly compensate their teams.

Social Focus

We focus investment on pressing needs such as energy, education, infrastructure, nutrition, healthcare, and financial services.

Values Oriented

While anyone can apply for funding, a venture must propose a plan that helps to accomplish a social purpose.
